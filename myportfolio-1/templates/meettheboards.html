We are the group of University of Michigan students organising the conference and making sure that the event is a great success every year! The board has roughly 60 students each year, including MBAs, BBAs, and other passionate undergraduates and graduate students interested in Asia business. We are divided into the Conference Group, which handles the internal finance, corporate relations, marketing, operations and, IT, and Panel Group, which is mainly responsible for contacting speakers and organising panel discussions.

The board has always been a close knit community, connecting students and alumnus with the same interests and goals. Throughout the school year, we collaborate and work hard to build the conference step by step. At the same time, we play hard and organise activities and events for board members. Our friendships go far beyond the successfully organised conference every year and are maintained even after our graduation.
